The adoption process

Adoption is easy! Our friendly adoption staff will work with you to find the right dog for your home.

Get started by checking our online adoption listings (updated every 20 minutes) or stop by one of our five adoption centers. We encourage you to spend time getting to know prospective pets. If you have questions or wish to visit with an animal, just ask a staff member or volunteer for assistance.
Once you have made a decision, we will sit down for an informal consultation to ensure that you and the pet you have selected are a good match. During this process we will also review the animals medical and behavioral records, explain policies and services available to adopters, and help you choose the right pet supplies.
We will also conduct random house checks monthly for one year, and then at intervals of six months to ensure our animals are sent only to the best homes.
We will continue to support you and your pet at home. Our online welcome kit for adopters is full of information that can make your pets transition from the shelter to your home as stress-free as possible, along with tips to keep your pet happy and healthy.
