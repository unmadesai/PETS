Thinking of adding a pet to your family?
Here are 5 reasons to adopt your new best friend.

1. Because you get free therapy

Dogs have been scientifically proven to be therapeutic animals, and aid in relieving stress and tension. They also make for great friends for children, teaching them unconditional love, friendship and creating a friendly and positive environment in the house.

2. Because you'll get a great animal

All animals sheltered at PETS are treated and vaccinated, to ensure they will not be of any danger to whichever home they go to.

3.Because you'll get a great bargain

You can now get a lifetime of unconditional love, loyalty and affection, completely free of cost. Also free of judgement, scorn, jealousy and envy. Only requires a lifetime supply of biscuits and cuddles.

4. Because you're environmentally responsible

By living in Mumbai, you are also partly responsible for the welfare of animals living in Mumbai, and this includes stray dogs.By adopting one, you are performing your moral responsibility towards society and the environment.

5. Because you'll save a life

By adopting a stray dog, you will be rescuing it from a miserable life of living on the streets, hunting for food, being in danger of being run over, and being bitten in fights with other dogs.
